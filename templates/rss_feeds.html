Benefits of RSS
  • No API key required
  • Works with most news sites

Setup Process
  1. Find the RSS feed URL for your favorite site
  2. Add it to this system with a descriptive name
  3. Assign it to a category that matches its content
Finding RSS Feeds
  • Look for RSS icon (orange square with white waves)
  • Add "/feed" to website URLs (e.g., example.com/feed)
  • Search "[site name] RSS feed" in Google
